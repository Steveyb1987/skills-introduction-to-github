About Us

Welcome to Finance Tracker, your ultimate tool for managing and tracking your financial transactions. Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their finances with ease and confidence.

Features

  • Track your daily expenses effortlessly.
  • View detailed summaries of your financial activity.
  • Organize transactions by categories like Food, Transport, and Entertainment.
  • Search and save your transactions for future reference.
  • Access your data anytime, anywhere.

How It Works

Finance Tracker is designed to be simple and intuitive. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter your transactions, including description, amount, and category.
  2. View a summary of your total transactions and spending.
  3. Search and filter transactions to analyze your spending habits.
  4. Save your data securely for future reference.

Why Choose Finance Tracker?

  • Gain insights into your spending habits.
  • Plan your budget effectively.
  • Save time with an easy-to-use interface.
  • Access your data securely from any device.
